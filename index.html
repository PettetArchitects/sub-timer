Help & gestures

Tap a player on the field to swap them. Tap another player to complete the swap.

Long-press a player on the field to start an injury sub. Then tap a bench player to bring on.

Tap anywhere else on the screen to cancel an armed swap or injury.

SUB button fires a sub immediately. RESET (paused) restores the half's clock and XI to kickoff (tap twice to confirm).

Drag the pitch to rotate, pinch to zoom. The View button (bottom-left of the pitch) cycles Behind / Side / Top presets.

The hamburger ☰ top-left holds Edit Team, Sub Settings, Settings and End game.
